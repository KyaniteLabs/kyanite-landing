Buying Questions

What has to be true before you pay?

The audit is for buyers who need product truth, not theater. These are the common fit checks.

Why lead with GitHub?

Because public repo truth is inspectable. It shows whether the tool, docs, tests, install path, and product promise actually line up.

What do I need to send?

The repo, current docs, install path, target user, what you think it should become, and what feels hard to explain or sell.

Do you only build MCP servers?

No. MCP is a strong pattern, but Kyanite also works on domain software, localization QA, video automation, repo diagnostics, and launch packaging.

What happens after the audit?

You leave with a productization plan. Kyanite can build, package, certify, or tell you the product is not ready yet.
